ezcli/cliprint

cliprint() utility function.

void cliprint(print_e type, char *prefix, char *fmt, ...);

general colored printing utility that autoinserts newlines and also takes custom prefixes. it also does printf-like % formatting.

print_e type has to be CLI_HINT, CLI_WARNING, or CLI_ERROR. these types of prints are colored blue, yellow, and red respectively, and also prefixed with HINT:, WARN:, and ERROR:.

setting the prefix to NULL will show these prefixes. setting it to CLI_EMPTY_PREFIX will show none.
